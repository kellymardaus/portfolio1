The Best Movies According to Kelly

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Emperor's New Groove

Kronk is blissfully happy. Kuzco learns to care about other human beings. Yzma as a cat always bring me joy.

Forgetting Sarah Marshall

Realizing who really cares for you and brings you joy is important. Dracula forver.

The grinch

The grumpiness and one liners in this movie are amazing. Grinch finds family at the end.
